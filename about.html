About Us

Al Hikma Islamic Online is an online Quran school based in Hargeisa, licensed by the Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs. Our school offers Islamic education, focusing on various aspects to cater to Somali families living abroad.

Why Choose Us

  • One-to-One Class: Each family or student is tutored by one teacher.
  • Convenient Timing: Select your convenient time and days for online Quran classes.
  • Reports & Certificates: Students receive progress reports and certificates upon course completion.
  • Flexible Timing: Choose the days and times that suit you best.
  • Expert Tutors: Our tutors are highly experienced and selected for their expertise.

Our Courses

  • Arabic Language: Learn Arabic in a short period.
  • Quran: Learn Quran recitation, tajweed, and tafseer.
  • Islamic Tarbiyah: Learn Islamic teachings and values.
